Installing the resource pack on Windows

1. Right click the Windows icon and press Run.
2. Write %appdata% and press OK.
3. Open .minecraft and then resourcepacks, and then drag the folder into the resourcepacks folder. Do NOT unzip the file.
4. Restart Minecraft.
5. On the main menu, click on Options, or whilst in-game click ESC and then Resource Packs. Select the resource pack with the Harlon logo and you're good to go!
