Welcome to Harlon.

A City-RP Minecraft server with a flourishing community and amazing player experiences, Harlon City Server (HCS) opened its doors to the public on the 14th of December 2019 with what is now known as Old Harlon. On the 17th of July 2021, we launched a brand new world, with significantly higher build quality and a new public transport network with realistic 3D trains and buses.

What makes Harlon unique?

HCS stands out from others in multiple ways. We offer experiences that aim to emulate real life, by giving the possibility to start proper companies with factories and shops, a stock market that follows the real life one, however using the Harlonian Pound and much more! We feature a wide variety of high quality, detailed builds. If you look closely enough, you may find a couple of easter eggs hiding around the server!

Need to find your way?

The server dynmap is the best way to navigate the city, find a player-owned shop, the nearest station, and many more things!
