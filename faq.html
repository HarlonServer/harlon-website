FAQs

Have questions? We have the answers!


Q: Where can I join your server?

A: You can join at play.harlonserver.net - Bedrock users can join at play.harlonserver.net:19132


Q: What Minecraft versions do you support?

A: We support Minecraft versions 1.17 and above.


Q: How do I see your 3D trains and buses?

A: First of all, make sure Resource Packs are enabled for Harlon City Server. Next, quit Minecraft and install Java. You can download it from the link below:

Download Java

Once you have downloaded the installer, open it and follow the prompts to install Java.

Then, install Optifine. You can download it from the link below:

info Note
If you're using an older Minecraft version than the one shown on Optifine's website, click "show all versions" to find the right Optifine download for your Minecraft version.

Download Optifine

Once you have downloaded the installer, open it and follow the prompts to install Optifine.

Finally, open the Minecraft launcher and press the upwards arrow next to the Minecraft version number, above the button. Click on , and then press to start Minecraft with Optifine. Enjoy!

Q: How do I sit inside a 3D train?

A: On the floor in the middle of each train carriage, there should be a grey rectangle. Right click that to sit. When you reach your destination, press Shift to get off, similar to dismounting from a horse or a minecart.
